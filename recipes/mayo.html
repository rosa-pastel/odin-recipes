Mayonnaise & Black Olives

Description

Delicious comfort food for when you don't have any ice cream.

Ingredients

  • Mayonnaise
  • Black Olives

Steps

  1. Cut the black olives.
  2. Put the olives in a bowl and add mayonnaise.
  3. Mix them together. Don't get your crush investigated by Dwight Schrute. Enjoy!
